Overtone Lab

As I studied physics of music and instruments, I inevitably came across the concept of overtones and their effect on timbre. Different combinations of frequency ratios and amplitudes create different waveforms and tuning systems. Overtone Lab gives the user two voices to customize, allowing for side-by-side comparison. By changing the frequency ratio of each partial, users can test custom tuning systems for dissonance and consonance in intervals.
