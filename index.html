Hello, my name is Charlie, known online as _cyxhe. I'm a student at Troy High School in Fullerton, California.

In middle school, I was a violin player in the string orchestra. When the COVID-19 pandemic forced schools to go virtual, the orchestra could no longer meet to rehearse. That was when our teacher introduced us to composition. They were arrangements of film scores, video game soundtracks, and classical music.
