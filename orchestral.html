Ashes of Light

A 3-part symphonic piece written to tell the story of stellar evolution.

Movement I is inspired by a main sequence star, full of nuclear fusion energy and ending its life in a supernova.

Movement II is inspired by a pulsar remnant, regularly emitting beams of radiation.

Movement III is inspired by the dark and mysterious black hole, drifting through the emptiness of space.
